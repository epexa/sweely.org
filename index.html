Frequently asked questions

Who's a Volunteer?
It’s an individual who provides assistance (work) in a social project for a Reward.
Who's a Sponsor?
It’s a legal entity or individual that provides goods or services on a non-refundable basis or for temporary use for a Reward.
Who's a Non-profit organization?
It’s a registered organization whose main activity is the creation of social projects and initiatives.
What are Resources?
These are goods and services provided by Sponsors for Rewards. They are located in the "Store" section.
What is a Reward?
This is a points of sweely.org web platform, which exists on the basis of blockchain for openness and security of transactions. Each resource in the Store section has its own value. You can only earn and spend Rewards on sweely.org platform.
How to earn Rewards?

If you're a Volunteer

ЗYou can earn Rewards by taking part in social projects from NPOs

If you're an NPO

You can earn Rewards by implementing your social project or initiative. When you complete the project, you receive Rewards calculated using our the secret formula of Sweely

The amount of Rewards depends on your activity on the Sweely platform, the creation of projects, the number of volunteers involved (scale), the success of the project completion, as well as feedbacks from volunteers.

If you're a Sponsor

You can earn Rewards by offering and publishing your Resources in the "Store" section. After your Resources are purchased by other users of the platform, you will receive the Rewards specified by you on your balance.

How to spend Rewards?
You can spend your earned Rewards by:

Purchasing the Resource in the "Store" section. Go to the "Store" page and choose the Resource you like within the limits of the amount of Rewards you have on your balance;

Transferring the Rewards to a social project or organization you like (give a donation).
